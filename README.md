OVERVEIW                       
===========================================================
Our team develoepa Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) designed to serve as a high-precision diagnostic tool to be used as a reliable second opinion. By utilizing machine learning to analyze complex patient data, clinicians can identify Alzheimer's in its earliest phases. Our system utilizes supervised learning architecture to analyze patient data and output a diagnosis. By incorporating SHAP, the model highlights the top five modifiable variables contributing to the patient's diagnoses, allowing targeted preventative care and clinical management. All these benefits would help give patients necessary time for clinical planning, insight to therapeutic intervention, as well as the preservation of long-term quality of life.
